Pre-Meeting

  • Define PurposeClear objective & expected outcomes
  • Set AgendaStructured timeline with priorities
  • Invite Right PeopleEssential stakeholders only
  • Share MaterialsPre-read documents & context
  • Book ResourcesRoom, equipment, tools
  • Set Ground RulesParticipation expectations

During Meeting

  • Start on TimeRespect everyone's schedule
  • Review AgendaConfirm scope & timing
  • Stay FocusedParking lot for off-topic items
  • Encourage ParticipationEqual voice for all attendees
  • Make DecisionsClear commitments & next steps
  • Manage TimeKeep discussions on track

Post-Meeting

  • Send SummaryKey decisions & action items
  • Assign OwnersClear accountability for tasks
  • Set DeadlinesSpecific timelines for delivery
  • Schedule Follow-upsTrack progress & next steps
  • Update SystemsDocument decisions & changes
  • Gather FeedbackContinuous improvement

Success Metrics

  • Decision Rate% of issues resolved
  • Action CompletionFollow-through on commitments
  • Time EfficiencyStart/end on schedule
  • Engagement LevelActive participation rate
  • ROIValue vs. time invested
  • SatisfactionParticipant feedback scores
